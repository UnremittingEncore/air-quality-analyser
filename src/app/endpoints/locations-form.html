General Health Tip

If you start to feel respiratory discomfort such as coughing or breathing difficulties, consider reducing the intensity of your outdoor activities. Try to limit the time you spend near busy roads, construction sites, open fires and other sources of smoke.

How Do We Calculate Air Quality?

In order to provide reliable and location-based air quality information. We fetch air quality parameters from an open source OpenAQ API on an hourly basis.
OpenAQ aggregates and harmonizes disparate air quality data into a single format so that more people can access the data for more impactful purposes.
